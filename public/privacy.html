Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

FlipTrack ("we", "our", or "the App") is a reseller inventory management tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use FlipTrack at fliptrack.live.

1. Information We Collect

When you use FlipTrack, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information — Email address and authentication credentials when you create an account.
  • Inventory Data — Item names, descriptions, prices, costs, images, SKUs, and other product details you enter.
  • Sales & Financial Data — Sale prices, fees, shipping costs, and expense records you input.
  • Third-Party Platform Data — When you connect marketplace accounts (e.g., eBay, Etsy), we receive and store OAuth tokens and listing/order data necessary to sync your inventory. We do not store your marketplace passwords.
  • Usage Data — Browser type, device information, and feature usage patterns to improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FlipTrack dashboard and its features.
  • Sync your inventory with connected marketplace platforms (eBay, Etsy, etc.).
  • Generate reports, analytics, and insights about your reselling business.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Communicate with you about your account or service updates.

3. Third-Party Integrations

FlipTrack integrates with third-party marketplace platforms including eBay and Etsy. When you authorize these connections:

  • We use OAuth 2.0 to securely connect — we never see or store your marketplace passwords.
  • We store encrypted access and refresh tokens to maintain your connection.
  • We access listing, inventory, and order data as permitted by the scopes you authorize.
  • You can disconnect any marketplace at any time from within the App, which revokes our access.

Please review the privacy policies of any third-party platforms you connect.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on Amazon Web Services) with row-level security policies. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. OAuth tokens for marketplace integrations are stored server-side and are never exposed to the browser.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party platforms you explicitly authorize (e.g., eBay, Etsy) to sync your inventory.
  • With service providers (e.g., hosting, authentication) who help us operate the App, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your account and associated data at any time.
  • Disconnect any third-party marketplace integration.
  • Export your data via CSV export features within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

FlipTrack uses browser local storage and IndexedDB to cache your inventory data for offline access and performance. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics trackers.

8. Children's Privacy

FlipTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, via in-app notification.
