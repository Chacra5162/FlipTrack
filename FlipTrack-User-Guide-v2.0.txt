2. GETTING STARTED
================================================================================

FlipTrack is a Progressive Web App (PWA) -- it works in your browser and
can be installed to your home screen for a native app experience. All data
syncs to the cloud via Supabase so you can access it from any device.

SIGN UP & LOGIN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Create an account
     Click the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner. Enter your email
     and a password, then verify your email address.

  2. Log in
     After verifying, log in with your credentials. Your account menu
     appears in the top-right showing your sync status.

  3. Install the PWA (optional)
     On mobile, tap "Add to Home Screen" from your browser menu. On
     desktop, look for the install icon in the address bar. This gives
     you a full-screen app experience.

NAVIGATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FlipTrack's 22 views are organized into three grouped dropdown menus in
the header, keeping the interface clean and uncluttered. Navigation groups
include ARIA expanded states for screen reader accessibility. Features
above your tier display a lock icon.

  Business:   Dashboard, Profit, Insights, Reports, Expenses, Tax Center,
              Period Compare, Platform ROI, Returns

  Inventory:  All Items, Supplies, Value Breakdown, Sourcing & Hauls,
              Inventory Health, Sourcing Analytics, Listing Scores,
              Margin Alerts

  Selling:    Sales Log, Crosslist, Shipping, Buyers

Click any group to reveal its views. The active group highlights in blue
so you always know where you are. On mobile, all views are accessible
from the bottom navigation bar's "More" popup.

TIP: Triple-tap the FlipTrack logo to load demo data -- great for
exploring features before adding your own inventory.

DASHBOARD WIDGETS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Six key metrics at a glance: inventory value, revenue, net profit,

  - Monthly Goals
    Set custom revenue and profit targets. Track your progress with

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The dashboard includes a Quick Export section for downloading CSV templates
and generating PDF reports. It also features a Backup & Restore panel
where you can download a full JSON backup of all your data (inventory,
sales, expenses, supplies) or restore from a previous backup file.

TIP: Download a JSON backup regularly for peace of mind. The backup
includes all item data, sales history, expenses, and supplies in a single
file you can restore at any time.

5. INVENTORY
================================================================================

Your complete product catalog. Every item you source, list, and sell is
managed here with rich filtering, sorting, inline editing, and bulk
operations.

FILTERING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The inventory view has a powerful multi-filter system. Combine any of
these filters to narrow down your catalog:

  - Platform
    Filter by any selling platform -- eBay, Etsy, Poshmark, Mercari,
    Amazon, Facebook Marketplace, Depop, and more.

    Filter by category, then drill into subcategory. Subcategory options

    In Stock, Low Stock, or Out of Stock. Quickly find items that need

  - Condition & Smoke
    Filter by item condition (NWT, NWOT, EUC, etc.) and smoke exposure

SORTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sort your inventory by name, price, cost, margin %, ROI, days listed,
or date added -- in ascending or descending order.

BULK OPERATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Select multiple items using checkboxes, then perform batch actions:

  - Bulk Delete
    Delete multiple items at once with undo capability. Items go to
    trash first.

  - Bulk Mark as Sold
    Record sales for multiple items simultaneously.

  - Bulk Price Edit
    Adjust prices for selected items by percentage or fixed amount.
    Preview changes before applying.

  - Bulk Category & Platform
    Reassign categories or change platform listings for multiple items
    at once.

INLINE EDITING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Click any price in the inventory table to edit it inline -- no need to
open the full editor. Use the +/- buttons next to the quantity field for
quick stock adjustments.

6. ADDING ITEMS
================================================================================

The Add Item modal is your primary way to get products into FlipTrack.
Access it from the "+ Add Item" button in the header or with Ctrl+N.

  1. Enter item details
     Name, category, subcategory. FlipTrack auto-detects Books for
     special fields (ISBN, Amazon rank, FBA calculator).

  2. Set pricing
     Enter your list price and cost. FlipTrack instantly shows expected
     profit and margin percentage.

  3. Choose platforms
     Select which platforms you'll list on. Platform-specific fees are
     automatically factored into profit calculations.

  4. Add attributes & photos
     Set condition, smoke exposure, color, size, brand, dimensions,
     and upload product photos with drag-and-drop.

TIP -- Prefill: Click "Prefill from Last" to copy details from your most
recently added item -- great when listing similar products in bulk.

BOOK MODE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When you select "Books" as the category, special fields appear: ISBN
lookup (auto-populates title, author, publisher), Amazon sales rank, FBA
fee calculator, and book-specific condition tags (Like New, Very Good,
Good, Acceptable, Reading Copy, Collectible). You can also scan ISBNs
with your device camera.

8. SALES LOG
================================================================================

Record every sale with platform, price, fees, and shipping costs.
FlipTrack automatically calculates your net profit per transaction.

  1. Mark an item as sold
     Click the cart icon on any inventory item, or use the "Mark as
     Sold" button in the drawer.

  2. Enter sale details
     Choose the selling platform, enter the sale price, quantity, and
     shipping cost. Fees are auto-estimated based on platform fee
     structures.

  3. Review & confirm
     The modal shows expected profit after fees. Choose per-unit or
     total pricing. Optionally back-date the sale.

BUNDLE SALES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Toggle "Bundle" in the sold modal to sell multiple items in a single
transaction. When enabled, you can select additional items from your
inventory. The total sale price auto-populates from the combined list
prices, and the revenue is split proportionally across items based on
their individual list prices. A red/green comparison shows how the
bundle price compares to the original combined total, so you can
instantly see the discount given.

BUYER ADDRESS (SHIP-TO)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The sold modal includes a collapsible "Ship-to Address" section with
fields for street, city, state, and ZIP code. When filled in, this
address auto-populates packing slips generated from the sale record,
saving time on fulfillment.

SALES FILTERING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Filter your sales history by date range and keyword search. The summary
totals (revenue, fees, profit) update to reflect only the filtered
results.

TIP: Sales automatically create buyer records. If the same buyer
purchases again, their profile is updated with cumulative data in the
Buyers CRM.

14. CROSSLIST DASHBOARD

OVERVIEW TAB

STATUS MATRIX
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A grid showing each item's listing status on each platform (Active,

  Item               eBay     Etsy      Poshmark   Mercari
  ----               ----     ----      --------   -------
  Nike Air Max 90    Active   Expired   --         Active
  Vintage Levi's     Active   Active    Active     --
  iPhone Case        --       Not Listed Active    Active

EBAY INTEGRATION (LIVE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

eBay is currently the only platform with full direct API integration.

  Connect eBay -> Pull Listings -> Push Updates -> Publish Live

Connect your eBay account via OAuth. FlipTrack can sync your active
listings, push new items to eBay, publish drafts live, and end listings
-- all from the Crosslist panel. All API calls have a 15-second timeout
with clear error messages if the connection is slow. On mobile, OAuth
uses a redirect flow instead of a popup for reliability. Failed auto-list
and crosslist cache operations now show toast notifications instead of
failing silently.

EBAY LISTING RECONCILIATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

eBay listings that are ended or deleted externally (directly on eBay, by
another tool, or expired) are automatically detected during sync and
marked as "ended" in FlipTrack. This keeps your Crosslist Status Matrix
accurate without manual cleanup.

FLIPTRACK AS PRICE SOURCE OF TRUTH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Price synchronization is one-directional: FlipTrack pushes prices to
eBay, never the other way around. When you update a price in FlipTrack,
it is automatically pushed to your eBay listing. This ensures FlipTrack
remains the single source of truth for pricing across all platforms.

AUTO-PUSH REPRICING TO EBAY & ETSY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Repricing rules now automatically push updated prices to both eBay and
Etsy when a rule fires. Previously, repriced items only updated locally
-- now the new price is synced to live listings on both platforms in
real time.

CROSS-PLATFORM AUTO-DELIST ON SALE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When a sale is detected through eBay or Etsy order sync, FlipTrack
automatically decrements the item quantity and -- if the item is fully
sold out (qty reaches 0) -- marks all other active platform listings
as "sold-elsewhere." This prevents overselling across platforms. The
same logic applies when you record a sale manually through the Sales
view.

  Sale detected (eBay/Etsy sync) -> Qty decremented -> Qty = 0?
    -> Auto-delist other platforms

ETSY INTEGRATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Etsy push is now live. When pushing a listing to Etsy, FlipTrack shows
a taxonomy picker (Etsy's required category tree) and a shipping profile
selector so your listings go live immediately instead of being created
as drafts.

Additional Etsy sub-tabs:

  - Shop Stats
    View favorites, total sold, active listings, and revenue summaries
    pulled from your Etsy shop.

  - Reviews
    Star rating average, distribution bar, and your 10 most recent
    reviews with rating and message.

  - Shipments
    View pending (unshipped) orders. Enter tracking numbers and carrier,
    then mark as shipped directly to Etsy.

  - Tags
    View and edit listing tags (max 13). Get AI-suggested tags based on
    title and category. Push updated tags to Etsy.

  - Photos
    Push product photos from FlipTrack to your Etsy listings via the API.

  - Price & Qty Sync
    Push price and quantity changes to Etsy. Bi-directional quantity sync
    keeps both systems in alignment.

WHATNOT LIVE SHOW TOOLS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

NOTE: Whatnot is not directly integrated via API. FlipTrack provides a

Plan and track your live shows: create show records, build item lineups

TEMPLATES & AUTO-RELIST
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Save listing templates per category with variables that auto-fill from

COPY & LIST (POSHMARK, DEPOP & MORE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For platforms without direct API integration -- Poshmark, Depop, Mercari,
Grailed, Facebook Marketplace, and others -- FlipTrack provides a
"Copy & List" workflow to make crosslisting fast and easy.

  Select Item -> AI Copy -> Text on Clipboard -> Open Platform
    -> Paste & Post

In the Crosslist Status Matrix, each item row shows action buttons per
platform:

  - AI Copy
    Generates a platform-optimized listing description using AI, tailored
    to the specific platform's style and format (e.g., hashtag-heavy for
    Poshmark, clean and concise for Depop). The text is automatically
    copied to your clipboard.

  - Copy Listing
    Copies your default listing text (or template-based text) to the
    clipboard -- title, condition, category, notes, and a shipping
    message.

  - Open Platform
    Opens the platform's listing creation page in a new tab (e.g.,
    poshmark.com/create-listing or depop.com/products/create). Just
    paste your text and upload photos.

  - Cycle Status
    Manually update the listing status (Active, Expired, Not Listed) to
    keep your Status Matrix accurate.

Step-by-step:

  1. Click "AI Copy" on the platform row
     FlipTrack generates a listing optimized for that platform and copies
     it to your clipboard.

  2. Click the open arrow to open the platform
     The platform's listing creation page opens in a new browser tab.

  3. Paste, add photos, and publish
     Paste your listing text (Ctrl+V), upload your product photos, set
     the price, and publish. Then cycle the status in FlipTrack to
     "Active" so your Status Matrix stays up to date.

BATCH LISTING: Select multiple items in the Inventory view and use
"Batch List" mode to generate listing text for all of them at once,
then work through each platform sequentially.

35. OFFLINE MODE & CLOUD SYNC
================================================================================

FlipTrack works fully offline. All data is stored locally in your
browser's IndexedDB. When you're online, changes sync to Supabase in
real-time for multi-device access.

HOW IT WORKS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Local Edit -> IndexedDB -> Cloud Sync -> All Devices

When you go offline, an amber banner appears at the top of the screen:
"You are offline -- data will sync when you reconnect." All changes queue
automatically. When connectivity returns, queued changes push to the
cloud and a toast notification confirms sync completion. A mutex-based
sync guard prevents concurrent sync operations from corrupting data. All
sync failures now display user-facing toast notifications (e.g., "Sync
failed -- changes saved locally, will retry" and "Cloud sync error --
your data is safe locally") so issues are never silent.

LOGOUT CLEANUP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Signing out now performs a comprehensive cleanup: all background sync
intervals (eBay/Etsy polling) are stopped, IndexedDB metadata is cleared,
localStorage data is purged, and realtime channel subscriptions are
removed. This ensures a clean state for the next session.

SYNC INDICATOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The sync icon in the header shows your current status: spinning during
sync, a checkmark when synced, or a warning badge when changes are
pending. The timestamp shows when data was last synced.

TIP: Install FlipTrack as a PWA for the best offline experience. The
Service Worker caches all app assets for instant loading even without
internet.

    (default: 20%). Critical alert if margin is negative.

  - Min Profit $
    Alert when dollar profit is below this amount (default: $5). Catches
    items where the margin percentage looks fine but actual dollars are
    too low.

  - Stale Days
    Flag items that have been listed longer than this many days
    (default: 60). Critical alert at 120+ days.

ALERT SEVERITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Alerts are color-coded by severity -- critical (red), high (orange), and
medium (yellow). Each alert shows the item name, the specific issue, and
links directly to the item editor so you can take action immediately.

NO-PRICE DETECTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Items with cost recorded but no listing price set get an automatic "high"
alert -- money is invested but the item isn't priced for sale.

NOTE: Margin Alerts use your current listing price, not comp data. If
you've repriced an item but the price field hasn't been updated, the
alert may not reflect the true margin.
