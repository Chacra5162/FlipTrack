Privacy Policy

Train App — Last updated April 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Train App ("the App") handles your personal information. The App is a personal workout tracking web application hosted at fliptrack.live/train.html.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information you voluntarily provide:

  • Workout data — exercises, sets, reps, and weights you log during training sessions
  • Session notes — any notes you type into the app
  • WHOOP fitness data — if you choose to connect your WHOOP account, we access your profile and workout history to log workouts on your behalf

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your workout program and track your progress within the App
  • To log completed workouts to your connected WHOOP account (only when you explicitly initiate this action)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes

3. Data Storage

Workout logs and session notes are stored locally in your browser's localStorage on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync it to WHOOP.

WHOOP OAuth tokens are stored in your browser's localStorage and are used solely to authenticate requests to the WHOOP API on your behalf.

4. WHOOP Integration

If you connect your WHOOP account, the App will:

  • Request access to read your profile information and log workouts
  • Only write data to WHOOP when you explicitly tap "Log to WHOOP"
  • Never read or store your WHOOP credentials (username/password)
  • Use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication — you authorize access directly through WHOOP's official login page

You can revoke WHOOP access at any time by visiting your WHOOP app settings and disconnecting the App.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies or any third-party analytics or tracking tools. No advertising networks have access to your data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
